NORTH INDIA

North India is known for its diverse culture, majestic Himalayan mountains, and the fertile plains of the Ganges and Yamuna rivers. It's a region of historical significance, with landmarks like the Taj Mahal in Agra and the holy city of Varanasi along the Ganges River.

EAST INDIA

East India is a region of diverse landscapes, from the dense forests of Odisha to the tea plantations in Assam. It's known for its cultural diversity, with states like West Bengal celebrated for its art and literature. Kolkata, the capital of West Bengal, is often called the "Cultural Capital of India." East India also offers a variety of mouthwatering dishes, including sweets like rasgulla and sandesh.

WEST INDIA

West India is a vibrant and colorful region known for its rich history, stunning deserts, and beautiful coastal areas. It includes states like Rajasthan, famous for its forts and palaces, and Maharashtra, home to bustling Mumbai. West India is also known for its spicy and flavorful cuisine, with dishes like vada pav and dhokla being popular choices.

SOUTH INDIA

South India boasts a unique culture, beautiful landscapes, and a rich tradition of art and music. The region includes states like Kerala, known for its backwaters and lush greenery, and Tamil Nadu, home to magnificent temples like the Meenakshi Temple in Madurai. South Indian cuisine is renowned for its use of coconut, rice, and spices, with dosa and idli being favorites.
